Welcome

2024

Welcome to Modish Deli, your premier dining destination in Fedha, Nairobi. We opened our doors this year with a vision to serve exquisite delicacies in a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Our Mission

At Modish Deli, we are committed to providing exceptional dining experiences through our carefully crafted menu, featuring both local and international delicacies prepared to perfection.

Location

Conveniently located in the heart of Fedha, Nairobi, we invite you to experience our unique blend of flavors and warm hospitality in a modern, sophisticated setting.

Our Services

Breakfast

Start your day with our hearty Kenyan breakfast featuring mandazi, chai, Spanish omelette, and traditional uji served fresh daily.

Nyama Choma

Savor our perfectly grilled meat selections served with kachumbari, ugali, and sukuma wiki prepared in authentic Kenyan style.

Traditional Dishes

Enjoy our selection of mukimo, githeri, pilau, and matoke stew made with locally sourced ingredients and traditional recipes.

Special Events

We cater for weddings, corporate events and parties with customized Kenyan and continental menu options.
